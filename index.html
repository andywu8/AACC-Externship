    About Us

    The Yale Asian American Cultural Center(AACC) has externships with organizations across the country. This spring break, we're externing with OneReality, a virtual reality company, and the National Japanese American Historical Society.

    Goal

    We're going to analyze the historical signifance of Japantown and integrate augmented reality with this to provide a forward way of thinking to have bustling shopping centers in the 21st century.

    Preparation

    To prepare, we need help from professionals especially in the field of community vitalization.

    01

    Retail

    Retail stores across the country are shutting down such as Abercrombie & Fitch, Macy's, Gap, and J.C Penney. This also affects small stores such as those in Japantown.

    02

    E-Commerce

    In 2019, retail e-commerce worldwide ammounted to 3.53 trillion US dollars and e-retail revenue are projected to grow to 6.54 trillion US dollars in 2022.

    03

    Japantown History

    Japantown was built and settled as part of the Western Addition neighborhood in the 19th and 20th century. Today Japantown is marked by its iconic peace plaza.

    04

    Shopping Experience

    To evolve, stores must combine physical and digital experiences. They must also improve the in-store experience as it is critical in incentivizing people to come.

    05

    Augmented Reality

    The augmented reality market is forecast to grow to 18.8 billion U.S. dollars in 2020.

    Mission

    Increase tourism

    We hope to rekindle interest in Japantown by emphasizing its rich culture through the use of augmented reality in seeing different sites.

    Educate

    Through our video and augmented reality, we want people to learn about the history of Japantown in a way that's interesting and interactive.

    Revitalize

    We want people to go to shops, have events in Japantown, and make this area a more vibrant space.

    Create

    We're creating a one of a kind augmented reality experience to interact with Japantown and will film a short film that explores the rich history of Japantown.

    Organize

    We'll organize an event at the end of our externship where we can debut our film as well as host speakers about the current state of Japantown.

    Solve Retail problems

    With the invention of Amazon, retail stores across the country are shutting down. To solve this, we're going to make Japantown an experience through the use of augmented reality.

    Project

    This spring break, we'll interview residents of Japantown and film a short documentary on Japantown. We want everyone's opinions, so we'll talk to historians, tourists, and locals. We'll debut our short film on Friday, March 13 at a Japantown get together. We will organize this event and have speakers, activities, workshops, and food.
